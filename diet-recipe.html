Recipe 1:

Greek Yogurt and Berry Parfait - Ingredients: - 1 cup Greek yogurt - 1 cup mixed berries (blueberries, strawberries, raspberries) - 1/2 cup granola - 1 tablespoon honey - Instructions: 1. Layer Greek yogurt, mixed berries, and granola in a bowl. 2. Drizzle with honey. 3. Serve chilled.

Recipe 2:

Grilled Chicken and Vegetable Wrap - Ingredients: - 1 boneless, skinless chicken breast - 1 cup mixed vegetables (cucumber, tomato, lettuce) - 1 whole wheat tortilla - 1 tablespoon hummus - Instructions: 1. Grill chicken breast and slice into strips. 2. Add mixed vegetables and hummus to the tortilla. 3. Add grilled chicken strips and wrap.
